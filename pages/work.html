WRITING

Editorial: The Hyphen - breaking, connecting, continuing - Artlink, 2024 (x). Co-written with Ava Lacoon and Hen Vaughan for the Hyphen issue of Artlink, which we guest edited together.

A copy is a copy is a copy - NGA, 2024 (x).
Made during my time in the NGA's Young Writers Digital Residency program. I try to discover something about Lindy Lee's photocopy works by copying her process of making them.

The Wiggly Worlds of Relax ‘em Ups – can videogames connect us with nature? - ACMI, 2023 (x).

A short wander through the (recent) history of visibility - Toolkits, 2022 (x).

HYPER//ECHO : a traveller's diary - ACMI, 2022 (x).
A multi-week project commissioned in tandem with Firepit Collective's virtual exhibition, which ran for 3 months. As the digital work grew and decayed, I would record my observations roughly every two weeks. I played around with the material where I could, publishing one essay as a little game and another as a HYPER//ECHO retrospective in the form of a Padlet.
